## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Transactional trade and position update
The engine SHALL record trades by calling `POST /api/v1/trades` on the platform API. The platform SHALL be responsible for persisting the trade and updating the corresponding position atomically on its side. The engine SHALL NOT perform a local database transaction for trade recording.

#### Scenario: Successful trade and position update
- **WHEN** a valid trade event is generated by the engine and `POST /api/v1/trades` returns 2xx
- **THEN** the platform SHALL have persisted the trade and updated the position, and the engine SHALL proceed with balance and Firestore risk state updates

#### Scenario: Position update fails
- **WHEN** `POST /api/v1/trades` returns a non-2xx, non-409 error
- **THEN** the engine SHALL log the error and skip the trade — it SHALL NOT retry indefinitely or block the signal consumer

## REMOVED Requirements

### Requirement: Trade event validation persistence to ledger_trades
**Reason**: The engine no longer writes to `ledger_trades` directly. Trade persistence is handled by the platform API (`POST /api/v1/trades`) which records to `tenant_trades`.
**Migration**: Trade history is available via `GET /api/v1/accounts/{id}/trades` on the platform API.
